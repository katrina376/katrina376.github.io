Hao-Yung (Katrina) Chan

I am a postdoc in NTUST. I develop mostly with Python and sometimes with HTML/CSS/JavaScript. Also, I am an advocator of open source. My public projects are listed in my GitHub profile. I have been working with M.-H. Tsai, Ph.D. since 2018 for furthur developments about the applications of conversational agents in civil engineering and disaster management.
